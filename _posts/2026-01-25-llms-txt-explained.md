You know how `robots.txt` tells Google which pages to crawl and which to ignore?

`LLMs.txt` does the same thing — but for AI models.

## What is LLMs.txt?

It's a simple text file you put on your website that tells AI models (ChatGPT, Gemini, Claude) what content they should reference when answering questions about your site.

## Why does it matter?

Right now, when someone asks an AI "What does [your company] do?", the model guesses based on whatever training data it has. With `LLMs.txt`, you hand it the exact answer.

## What goes in it?

```
# This tells the AI about your site
Title: Your Company Name
Description: What you actually do
Section: Key product or service
- Bullet point with details
- Another point

Section: Pricing
- Our plans start at...
```

## The bottom line

SEO is evolving from ranking in Google to ranking in AI answers. `LLMs.txt` is the easiest way to start.
